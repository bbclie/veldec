Our Story

The obsession with detail
that transforms

VelDec was born from a quiet dissatisfaction. After leading digital projects across Brazil, Portugal, England and the United States, founder Bárbara Declie realized something: the market offered volume — but rarely precision.

So we built something different: a digital boutique that refuses mediocrity as a standard and treats every project as a singular work. We don’t work with everyone — we deliberately choose who we build with.

From complex systems to growing businesses

Our experience comes from demanding digital environments — and that taught us something valuable: small businesses deserve the same level of clarity, strategy and positioning as enterprise brands.

Selection is part of the service

We accept a maximum of seven projects per month — not by limitation, but by choice. This allows us to deeply understand each client and deliver with obsessive attention.

Results are the only metric

Beautiful websites that don’t convert are expensive decoration. Every design decision and every line of code exists to elevate your brand perception and generate measurable results.
